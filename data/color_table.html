Types of Colors

Colors can be divided into three categories:

  1. Three Primary Colors: Blue, Red, Yellow
  2. Three Secondary Colors: Orange, Green, Violet
  3. Six Tertiary Colors: Yellow-Green, Blue-Green, Red-Orange, Yellow-Orange, Blue-Violet, Red-Violet.
